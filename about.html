We Are Members of the St Theresa Family

We are members of the St. Theresa family—a united community of faith, love, and service. Rooted in the teachings of Christ and inspired by the example of St. Theresa, we journey together in prayer, fellowship, and devotion to God. Our parish is more than a place of worship; it is a spiritual home where every member is welcomed, valued, and encouraged to grow in faith. As one family in Christ, we support one another, share in one another’s joys and challenges, and commit ourselves to serving God and humanity. Through worship, sacraments, outreach, and active participation in parish life, we strive to live out the Gospel and be a light to our community and beyond. Together, as members of the St. Theresa family, we continue to build a church founded on faith, hope, love, and unity.

Our Purpose.

Our purpose is to glorify God by nurturing a strong community of faith rooted in love, prayer, and service. Guided by the teachings of Christ and the example of St. Theresa, we are committed to spiritual growth, unity among our members, and compassionate outreach to all. Through worship, the sacraments, and acts of charity, we seek to live the Gospel and be a beacon of hope in our community.

Our Mission.

To share the love of Christ by growing in faith, worshipping together, and serving others with compassion. Inspired by St. Theresa, we strive to build a welcoming community where everyone is nurtured, united, and empowered to live the Gospel every day.

“Above all, love each other deeply, because love covers over a multitude of sins.”

1 Peter 4:8 NIV

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

We live our mission and serve our community guided by these core values: Faith – Placing Christ at the center of our lives and growing in spiritual maturity. Love – Showing compassion, forgiveness, and care for all. Unity – Working together to strengthen our parish and community. Service – Serving others through charity, outreach, and meaningful involvement. Integrity – Living honestly and humbly, honoring God in all we do.

Church Is Family

At St. Theresa Catholic Church, we believe that church is more than a building—it is a family. Here, every member is welcomed, valued, and cherished. Just as a family shares in each other’s joys and supports one another through challenges, we walk together in faith, prayer, and service. Our parish family is united by Christ’s love, strengthened by fellowship, and inspired to grow spiritually together. Through worship, the sacraments, and acts of kindness, we nurture a community where everyone feels at home, connected, and empowered to live out the Gospel every day. Because here, you are not just a member—you are family.

Catholic Culture

Catholic culture is a rich tapestry of faith, tradition, and community that shapes the way we worship, live, and serve. At St. Theresa Catholic Church, we honor the teachings of Christ, the guidance of the Church, and the example of the saints as we grow in holiness together. Our culture is expressed through prayer, the sacraments, music, liturgy, and celebration of feast days—creating a vibrant and nurturing environment for spiritual growth. It emphasizes love, compassion, service, and a commitment to justice, reflecting the life of Christ in every action. In embracing Catholic culture, we connect with a timeless tradition that unites believers across the world, strengthens our faith, and inspires us to live out the Gospel with devotion, joy, and purpose.

Spirit Led Life

Living a Spirit-led life means allowing the Holy Spirit to guide our thoughts, decisions, and actions every day. At St. Theresa Catholic Church, we encourage our members to seek God’s direction through prayer, reflection, and discernment, trusting that the Spirit empowers us to live according to Christ’s teachings. A Spirit-led life is marked by love, wisdom, humility, and service. It inspires us to walk in faith, embrace God’s purpose, and share His grace with others. By following the Spirit’s guidance, we grow closer to God, strengthen our community, and become a living witness of Christ’s love in the world.


What We Believe.

The Creed.

I believe in one God, the Father almighty, maker of heaven and earth, of all things visible and invisible. I believe in one Lord Jesus Christ, the Only Begotten Son of God, born of the Father before all ages. God from God, Light from Light, true God from true God, begotten, not made, consubstantial with the Father; through him all things were made. For us men and for our salvation he came down from heaven, and by the Holy Spirit was incarnate of the Virgin Mary, and became man. For our sake he was crucified under Pontius Pilate, he suffered death and was buried, and rose again on the third day in accordance with the Scriptures. He ascended into heaven and is seated at the right hand of the Father. He will come again in glory to judge the living and the dead and his kingdom will have no end. I believe in the Holy Spirit, the Lord, the giver of life, who proceeds from the Father and the Son, who with the Father and the Son is adored and glorified, who has spoken through the prophets. I believe in one, holy, catholic, and apostolic Church. I confess one Baptism for the forgiveness of sins and I look forward to the resurrection of the dead and the life of the world to come. Amen.
