Welcome to St Theresa
Catholic church Osubi

Delta State, Nigeria St. Theresa Catholic Church, Osubi, is a faith-filled Catholic community dedicated to the worship of God and inspired by the life and teachings of St. Theresa of the Child Jesus. Located in Osubi, Delta State, our parish serves as a spiritual home where believers gather to grow in faith, hope, and love. We are committed to the celebration of the Holy Eucharist, the administration of the sacraments, and the spiritual formation of our parishioners through prayer, sound teaching, and active participation in parish life. Our liturgical celebrations are reverent, vibrant, and centered on Christ. At St. Theresa Catholic Church, we believe in living out the Gospel beyond the walls of the church. Through charity, evangelization, youth and family programs, and community outreach, we strive to promote peace, unity, and service to humanity in line with the teachings of the Catholic Church. Guided by devoted clergy and supported by committed lay faithful, our parish continues to grow as a welcoming community where everyone is encouraged to encounter Christ, deepen their faith, and serve others with love. You are warmly welcome to worship with us.
