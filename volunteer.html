Join A Society

Discover a deeper sense of belonging by joining one of our parish societies. Each society is a community of faith where members come together to pray, serve, and support one another while contributing to the life and mission of the Church. Joining a society allows you to use your gifts, develop spiritually, and build strong relationships rooted in shared values. No matter your age or background, there is a society where you can grow, serve, and feel at home. Take the next step—get involved, grow in faith, and become an active part of our parish family.

Grow Spiritually Catholic societies provide opportunities for prayer, formation, and deeper understanding of the faith, helping you strengthen your relationship with God. Build Community Joining a society connects you with fellow parishioners who share your values, offering friendship, support, and a true sense of belonging within the Church. Serve with Purpose Through active service and outreach, societies allow you to use your talents to support the Church and make a positive impact in the wider community.


Begin your journey into the Catholic faith by joining a society today

Alter Knight.

Joining the Altar Knights is a special way for young children to serve God and grow closer to Him. As an Altar Knight, you learn how to assist during Mass, understand the meaning of the prayers, and take part in the sacred life of the Church from an early age. Being an Altar Knight helps you grow in discipline, confidence, and responsibility. It teaches respect for the altar, obedience, teamwork, and good character—values that guide you both in church and in everyday life. Most of all, it is a joyful experience. You make new friends, feel proud to serve at the altar, and discover that even at a young age, you have an important role in God’s family.

Alter girls.

Altar Girls who dress the altar play a beautiful and reverent role in the life of the Church. Through their service, they help prepare the sacred space for Holy Mass, ensuring that everything is set with care, order, and respect for God’s presence. This ministry teaches young girls the values of responsibility, attentiveness, and reverence. By assisting behind the scenes, they learn that serving God can be done quietly and faithfully, offering their time and effort as an act of love and worship. Dressing the altar also helps them grow spiritually, deepen their understanding of the Mass, and feel a strong sense of belonging within the Church community. It is a meaningful way for young girls to serve God with humility, devotion, and joy.

Church Choir.

The Church choir is a vital part of our worship, leading the congregation in praise and lifting hearts to God through sacred music. Through song, the choir helps create a prayerful atmosphere during Mass and other liturgical celebrations, making worship more meaningful and inspiring. Being part of the choir is both a ministry and a calling. Members grow spiritually as they use their voices and talents to glorify God, while also learning discipline, teamwork, and dedication. It is a place where faith and music come together in service. The choir is open to all who love to sing and serve. By joining, you become part of a united group that worships God, enriches the liturgy, and strengthens the faith of the entire parish community.

Church Warden.

The Church Warden plays an important role in maintaining order, reverence, and hospitality within the church. Through their service, wardens help ensure that worship takes place in a peaceful, organized, and welcoming environment for all. As guardians of church order, Church Wardens assist with seating, offerings, crowd control, and the general care of the church space during services and events. Their presence reflects discipline, responsibility, and a deep respect for the house of God. Serving as a Church Warden is a ministry of dedication and leadership. It offers members the opportunity to serve the Church faithfully, support the clergy, and contribute to the smooth running of parish activities while growing in commitment and service to God.

Catholic Men Organizatio (CMO).

The Catholic Men Organization (CMO) is a society dedicated to forming strong men of faith, integrity, and service within the Church and society. It provides a space for Catholic men to grow spiritually, strengthen their family life, and live out their faith with purpose and responsibility. Through prayer, fellowship, and active service, CMO members support the mission of the Church and contribute to parish development and community outreach. The organization encourages leadership, unity, and commitment to Catholic values in everyday life. Joining the CMO is an opportunity for men to deepen their relationship with God, support one another in faith, and serve the Church as responsible leaders and role models.

Catholic Women Organization (CWO).

The Catholic Women Organization (CWO) is a vibrant society that brings together women of faith to grow spiritually, support one another, and serve the Church and wider community. It plays a vital role in nurturing family life, parish unity, and Christian values. Through prayer, formation, and charitable works, CWO members contribute actively to parish activities and outreach, offering their time, talents, and resources in service to God. The organization also encourages fellowship, leadership, and personal growth among women. Joining the CWO provides women with an opportunity to deepen their faith, build meaningful relationships, and make a positive impact in the Church and society.

CYON.

The Catholic Youth Organization Nigeria (CYON) is a dynamic group for young people who want to grow in faith, serve the Church, and make a positive impact in their communities. CYON provides a space for youth to develop spiritually, socially, and morally while learning leadership, teamwork, and responsibility. Through prayer, Bible studies, retreats, and community service, CYON members actively participate in the life of the Church and witness their faith in everyday life. The organization also fosters fellowship, mentorship, and personal growth among young Catholics. Joining CYON is an opportunity to strengthen your relationship with God, make lifelong friends, and play an active role in building a vibrant, faith-filled community.

Sacred Heart of Jesus.

The devotion to the Sacred Heart of Jesus is a beautiful expression of love, mercy, and compassion. It honors the heart of Christ, which burns with love for all humanity, showing His deep desire to bring souls closer to God. Through this devotion, the faithful are called to reflect on Christ’s infinite love, repent for sins, and grow in holiness. It encourages prayer, acts of reparation, and a commitment to love God and neighbor sincerely. By embracing the Sacred Heart of Jesus, we are reminded that God’s love is constant, tender, and ever-patient, inviting each of us to trust Him, seek His mercy, and live a life rooted in His love.
