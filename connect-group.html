Join The Catechism Class to Begin Your journey

Joining the Catechism class is the first step in deepening your understanding of the Catholic faith. Whether you are a child, youth, or adult, the class provides structured guidance in Scripture, Church teachings, sacraments, and Christian living. Through lessons, discussions, and prayer, you will grow in knowledge, strengthen your faith, and be better prepared to live as an active member of the Church. The Catechism class is not just learning—it’s a journey of faith that equips you to follow Christ with confidence and love.

At St. Theresa Catholic Church, Osubi, our Catechism classes are designed to guide children, youth, and adults in growing closer to God and understanding the Catholic faith. Each class helps parishioners learn about the Bible, the Sacraments, and how to live as faithful members of the Church. Our Classes Include: Children’s Classes (Ages 5–12) Introducing basic prayers, Bible stories, and Catholic teachings, helping young children develop a strong foundation in faith. Youth/Teen Classes (Ages 13–17) Focused on deeper understanding of the faith, moral formation, and preparation for Confirmation and other sacraments. Adult Catechism (RCIA / Adult Faith Formation) For adults learning about the Catholic faith, returning to the Church, or preparing for sacraments such as Baptism, Confirmation, or First Communion. At St. Theresa, every class is a journey of faith—helping parishioners grow spiritually, build community, and live the teachings of Christ in their daily lives.
